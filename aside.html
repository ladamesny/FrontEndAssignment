<aside> tag

Used for section of a page consisting of content that is tangentially related to the conted around the <aside> element, and which should be considered separate from that content. Such sections are often represented as sidebars in printed typography

Below I used an aside tag to describe the city of Paris.

I went to Paris for the first time in 2012. I met my wife's family and had a great time. I'm looking forward to visiting again.

Paris, France

Paris is the capital and most populous city of France.
